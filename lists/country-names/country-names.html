105. Macedonia

Republic of Macedonia. This is the name used by the state itself and is used by the UK for all bilateral purposes. The name used by the UK when formally establishing diplomatic relations was “The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia”, and this is used in multilateral contexts. The designation used by the United Nations to refer to Macedonia is “The former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia”, listed under “T” alphabetically.
